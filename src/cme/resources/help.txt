CME Tracker Help

Add an entry by entering a date, title, credit type, category, and number of hours, then choose Add.

Dates use yyyy-mm-dd format, such as 2026-04-18.

Select a row in the table to load it into the form. After editing the fields, choose Update.

Use Start and End at the bottom of the window to filter the table and report to a license cycle or any other date range.

The report totals hours by category and also shows a simple osteopathic target check:
100 total hours with at least 20 AOA Category 1A hours.

Your records are saved in data/cme-records.csv.
